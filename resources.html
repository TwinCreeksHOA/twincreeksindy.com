Paint Information

Listed below are the Fieldstone and Brookstone house colors that are approved by the Board per original house specifications. Any restoration or repair of the exterior of a home shall be performed substantially in accordance with the Covenant Declaration and the original plans and specifications for the same, unless other action is approved by the Architectural Committee. Any repair or alteration that is not in accordance with the original house plans must be pre-approved by sending in an Architectural Approval Form. Please email the Board with any questions prior to beginning repairs or alterations.

Mailbox Information

Your mailbox is often the first impression someone gets of your house, so make it a positive one by keeping it in top form and when the time comes, replacing it.

Suppliers

TCHOA contracts with Address Art and Mailbox Solutions for Mailboxes and Mailbox Posts. The price for our community is $66 plus tax and $15 for installation. Please feel free to email the Board if you have any questions or need additional information.

Address Art supplies custom mailboxes. Should you have questions regarding mailbox standards or maintenance, call 317-254-1508, email mailbox@addressart.com, or visit the website at www.addressart.com.

Twin Creeks Neighborhood Service Providers
