Paint Information:

Listed below are the Fieldstone and Brookstone house colors that are approved by the Board per original house specifications. Any restoration or repair of the exterior of a home shall be performed substantially in accordance with the Covenant Declaration and the original plans and specifications for the same, unless other action is approved by the Architectural Committee. Any repair or alteration that is not in accordance with the original house plans must be pre-approved by sending in an Architectural Approval Form. Please email the Board with any questions prior to beginning repairs or alterations.

Mailbox Information:

TCHOA contracts with Address Art for Mailboxes and Mailbox Posts. The price for our community is $66  plus tax and $15 for installation. You can also contact Joe Peeler at 255-2500 for post installation. Please feel free to email the Board if you have any questions or need additional information.
