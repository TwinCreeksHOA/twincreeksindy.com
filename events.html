Twin Creeks is a wonderful community with many active residents. We are in the process of planning exciting social activities throughout the year for both children and adults, but we need participation. If you are interested in any of the following and would like to volunteer, please contact a Board Member. The TCHOA will provide the necessary funds, we need you to provide the ideas and planning! Activities and current ideas include:

Easter Egg Hunt
The Easter Bunny would like to make a special trip to Twin Creeks the weekend prior to Easter to hide candy-filled eggs in the common areas. Children hunt eggs for prizes and have a special visit with the Easter Bunny.
July 4th Fire Truck Parade
Patriotism and pride prevail at the Annual 4th of July Parade. An option to have the local Fire Department lead a parade through the streets with bells and whistles. Our residents follow with decorated bikes, scooters, motorized cars, and waving flags followed by ice cream and watermelon at the playground.
Halloween Celebration
A ghoulish time is had by all each Halloween for residents of all ages. From pumpkin carving and treats to costume parades through the streets of Brookstone and Fieldstone, families enjoy fun and spooky times together.
Breakfast with Santa
Children get a visit from Santa Clause while enjoying early morning breakfast snacks. Each child receives a wrapped gift from Santa.
Holiday Social
A great time for neighbors to spread holiday cheer. A roaring fire is the backdrop to this festive occasion which includes drinks, snacks, and music.
Christmas Eve Neighborhood Luminary Display
A lovely display lights up the streets of Brookstone and Fieldstone at dusk each Christmas Eve. Neighbors fill white paper bags with sand and candles for a glorious glow throughout the neighborhood or simply use their own creativity in lighting their home.
Christmas Caroling
A fun tradition that brings neighbors together and helps get everyone in the Holiday Spirit.
Neighborhood Garage Sales
This is a great opportunity to turn "trash into treasures". We are currently thinking of planning a Spring and Fall Garage Sale.
Neighborhood Groups and Clubs
This is a great opportunity for neighbors to get together and have a fun night out. There are many fun ideas such as: euchre, bongo, book clubs, supper clubs, golf outings just to name a few.

This is just an example of some of the areas we would like to concentrate on. We need your ideas and participation. Send us an email or give us a call. We look forward to hearing your thoughts!

Yard of the Month

During Spring and Summer, homeowners work hard to earn this honor. Winners receive a yard sign to display in their front yard. Look for our newest winners coming this Spring as you drive through the neighborhood!

We Need You!

We are in need of volunteers to help with various committees and neighborhood events. It's a great way to get involved, improve your neighborhood and meet people. If you are interested in any of the events at the bottom of this page, please send us an email.
