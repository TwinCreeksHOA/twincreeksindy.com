Budget Updates

May 2009 - Year To Date Expenditures

2008 Final Budget (Amended)

Linked below is the 2008 year end budget. There was a shortfall of $12,396.71 which resulted in the Board having to use reserve funds to cover these expenses.

The factors which caused the shortfall were:

  • Tree removal and trimming due to the wind storm in the spring.
  • Preventative erosion maintenance of the ponds exceeded budget.
  • Higher landscaping costs due to fuel and fertilizer prices.
  • Reimbursable fees for legal services not collected.
  • Reimbursable fees for lawn maintenance of vacant and/or delinquent homeowners that were not collected.
  • Annual dues not received due to vacant, foreclosure or bankrupt properties.

Several of these items have been addressed in the 2009 budget. In addition, all service contracts are being re-negotiated in an attempt to maintain a balance budget for 2009. If you have any questions please contact either the secretary or treasurer.
